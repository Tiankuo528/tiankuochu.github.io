Selected Work

My work focuses on developing and deploying robust machine learning systems, from large-scale data pipelines and MLOps infrastructure to novel foundation models for medical diagnosis. Below are a couple of highlights.

GraphRAG + LORA System @ Dow

Designed and deployed a production-grade LLM pipeline for root-cause analysis, featuring a LoRA-fine-tuned LLaMA 3-70B model with a Graph-based RAG system. The full MLOps system was deployed on an H100 HPC cluster, achieving a 45% reduction in latency while serving over 1,000 queries per hour.

BoneMet: Medical Foundation Model

Led the creation of BoneMet, the first AI-ready, open-source multimodal dataset for Breast Cancer Bone Metastasis, securing a \$300K NIH grant. I engineered a novel Spatial-Temporal Aligned Vision Transformer (STA-ViT) and fine-tuned a Multimodal Vision-Language Model (LLaVA), resulting in a first-author paper at ICLR 2025.
